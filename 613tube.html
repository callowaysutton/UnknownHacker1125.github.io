What is 613 Tube?

Watching YouTube videos on 613Tube

Watch any YouTube video on 613Tube and you'll get to see the video you want, without any advertisements inside and around the video area. No related video suggestions or comments there to drift you away. Just get the video you want, nothing else.

From now on, anytime someone sends you a YouTube link, just replace "youtube.com" with "613tube.com", and you will see the same video, just in a cleaner atmosphere.

Example: https://www.613tube.com/watch/?v=LXQcrsS1PzM

Also, before sending a YouTube link to a friend, be kind enough to correct the link so that he too will get to fully enjoy the video in the clean atmosphere.

613Tube Video Search

Now you can search YouTube through 613Tube. 613Tube uses the regular YouTube search engine, with YouTube's SafeSearch feature automatically enabled and our very own Community-Based Filtering system.

Community-Based Filtering

613Tube users (like you) are constantly improving our advanced video filtering system. Whenever an inappropriate search result shows up, just click the "Red Flag" at the corner of your screen to remove a video from future searches.

613Tube Browser Extensions

To make life even easier, you can install the 613Tube Extension for your browser. Once installed, all YouTube links will automatically be redirected to 613Tube. You will then be able to confidently click on any YouTube link without needing to manually replace "youtube" with "613tube".

Additionally, with the extension installed, even YouTube videos which are embedded into other websites will be modified to not display any video suggestions when the video finishes playing.
